WHO WE ARE

Newvasha Development EPZ Limited is an Export Processing Zone (EPZ) located in Naivasha, Kenya. As an EPZ, we work to develop and promote industrial and economic activities within this designated geographic area. To carry out our commitment, we partner with dynamic businesses that focus on export-oriented production.

The creation of Newvasha started with recognizing a need. A company approached our ownership as they were searching for a location to produce export products. Other EPZs in Kenya were not viable options, as many are located in dis organized, chaotic urban areas with a lack of physical space and strict requirements for export production.

As a privately owned EPZ strategically located in a spacious and affordable area, Newvasha addresses these challenges. We offer the flexibility to purchase a plot, lease space, or build to a business specifications with plenty of support and opportunities for growth.

OUR MISSION

At Newvasha EPZ Limited, our mission is a bold one. We are dedicated to not only promoting the growth of the companies within the EPZ, but also contributing to the countrys greater economic growth. We believe our suc cess lies in creating lasting value for both the businesses and the local community. In partnership with the EPZs thriving companies, we aim to create more job opportunities in Kenya and develop the skills of workers within the region.
